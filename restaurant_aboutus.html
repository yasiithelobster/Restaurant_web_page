About Us

Nestled in the vibrant heart of the city, our establishment offers a truly enchanting culinary journey, where the rich and diverse traditions of Sri Lanka and India converge beautifully. We present an authentic yet contemporary dining experience, drawing inspiration from the deep-rooted flavors of both nations.

Whether you seek a revitalizing morning repast featuring local staples, a satisfying midday indulgence with subcontinental flair, or a comforting evening meal that harmoniously blends culinary worlds, our diverse offerings cater to every occasion

Our passionate and innovative culinary team brings their expertise to every creation, artfully weaving together indigenous spices with global influences, ensuring the use of only the most premium ingredients. From the comforting warmth of island-inspired curries to the bold and aromatic notes of tandoor-cooked specialties. we are dedicated to crafting a truly memorable dining experience with each and every taste.

With a welcoming ambiance that echoes the lively cultures of both Sri Lanka and India, coupled with exceptional hospitality and a menu that celebrates this delightful fusion. our restaurant provides the perfect setting to connect with loved ones, friends, or colleagues.

We invite you to visit us and discover why we are the ideal choice for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and all the moments in between. We eagerly anticipate the pleasure of serving you an unforgettable dining experience that will transport you through the captivating flavors of Sri Lanka and India!
